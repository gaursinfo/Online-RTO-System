Welcome ....

The Transport Department of Goverment of Uttar Pradesh is entrusted with the responsibility of providing an efficient public transportation system, control of vehicular pollution, registration of vehicles in Uttar Pradesh, issuance of Driving licences, issuance of various permits, collection of road taxes. The department also entrusted in policy-making, co-ordination, implementation, monitoring and regulatory functions of all the Transport related aspects of Uttar Pradesh.

Starting in RTO Lucknow on 8th January 2015 as a pilot project, this system allows applicants to submit LL / DL forms online and make online fee payment. They can then come during working hours to the transport office for bio-metrics and other required formalities, or even book a particular online time-slot of their choice to do so. Guidelines of the process are given in Hindi and English. The online DL application system is available at https://sarathi.nic.in .

This system has been now extended to Kanpur, Allahabad & Varanasi, and will be extended to other districts in a phase-wise manner.

This system allows vehicle dealers selling new private vehicles to complete initial registration documentation and pay due taxes / fees directly into the government treasury through online payment gateway. Vehicle dealers in these districts may contact their respective RTO / ARTO for details and user ID / password. Please access the portal at https://vahan.up.nic.in/vahaninweb/faces/registration/jsp/index.jsp
